How to Build your own Water Bottle Rocket

What you'll need:

  • 1 Empty 2 Liter Bottle
  • 1 Bic Pen
  • 1-2 Sheet(s) of sturdy construction paper
  • 1 Bicycle Pump
  • 1 Sheet of cardboard
  • 1 Roll of Duct Tape
  • Water
  • Drill
  • Saw

Steps:

  1. Open the bottle and save the cap
  2. Disassemble the Bic pen.
  3. Saw the barrel of pen in half.
  4. Drill a hole in the cap the size of the barrel.
  5. Attach the pen barrel to the bottle cap.
  6. Use the sturdy construction paper to create a nose cone.
  7. Cut out the cardboard to create the fins.
  8. Attach the nose cone to the head of the bottle and the fins to the lower half of the rocket body using duct tape.
  9. Connect the outer end of the pen barrel to a bicycle pump.
  10. Fill the bottle with water.
  11. Screw on the cap; your water bottle rocket is ready to launch
  12. Pump air through the bicycle pump and watch your rocket fly.
